Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026 · Developer: ParthSarthi · App package: com.parthsarthi.littlearjuns

Our promise, in plain words

Little Arjuns is made for children aged 3–8. We collect no data. We show no ads. We track no one. Everything your child does in the app stays on your phone — it is never sent to us or to anyone else.

1. Information we collect

None. Little Arjuns: Learn and Grow does not collect, store on our servers, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or user data — from children or from adults. Specifically, the app does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact details
  • Photos, videos, audio recordings, or files
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics, crash reports containing personal data, or behavioural profiles
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

The app has no account system. There is nothing to sign up for and no password to create.

2. How the app works without your data

Learning progress (for example, stars earned or games completed) is saved only on your own device, so the app can greet your child where they left off. This information never leaves the phone. Uninstalling the app erases it completely.

3. Voice features

Little Arjuns speaks to your child using your device's built-in text-to-speech voice. The app does not use the microphone, does not listen, and does not record audio. Speech goes one way only: from the app to your child.

4. Advertising and third parties

The app contains no advertising of any kind and integrates no third-party advertising or analytics software. We do not share data with third parties, because we do not have any data to share.

5. Children's privacy

Little Arjuns is designed for children and their families. We take children's privacy laws seriously, including the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP). Because the app collects no personal information from any user, no personal information is ever collected from children. The app participates in the Google Play Designed for Families programme and is committed to the Google Play Families Policy.

6. Internet use

The app uses an internet connection to load its learning content. No personal information is transmitted in doing so. The app contains no links that take children outside the app experience, other than parent-facing pages such as this policy.

7. Purchases

The app is currently free, with no in-app purchases. If an optional paid unlock for older age groups is introduced in the future, it will be processed entirely by Google Play's own billing system behind a parental gate — we will still receive and store no personal information, and this policy will be updated before any such change.

8. Security

The safest data is the data that is never collected. Since Little Arjuns gathers nothing, there is no database of your family's information anywhere that could be lost, leaked, or misused.

9. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles information, we will update this page and its effective date before the change takes effect. Material changes will be highlighted clearly.
